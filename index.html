About India

India is a vast and diverse country, home to over 1.4 billion people. It's the cradle of one of the world's oldest civilizations and a land where ancient traditions blend seamlessly with modern innovations. India is the seventh-largest country by land area and boasts a wide variety of landscapes, climates, and ecosystems.

From the towering Himalayas in the north to the sun-kissed beaches in the south, India's geography is breathtaking. The Thar Desert in the west contrasts with the lush forests of the northeast. Major rivers like the Ganges, Yamuna, and Brahmaputra sustain millions of lives.
